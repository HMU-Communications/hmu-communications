About/Company History

About Us/Vision/Values

HMU Communications is a medical communications agency available for users all over the world. Our goal is to expertly serve medical professionals and our clients.

We believe that nothing should stand in the way of receiving healthcare, and with us, flexibility and dedication meet, allowing you to be only a click away from seeing a carefully chosen physician.

Our goal is to help people lead healthy, happy lives by providing them with access to top healthcare from highly qualified professionals, on demand. Strong communication is not only vital to the satisfaction of our consumers, but also one of our core values. We put the interests of our clients, suppliers, and team members first.

Founded in 2021, our team specializes in health economics, outcomes communications, publications, and training.

We have received recognition for being leaders in innovative healthcare communications, and strive to become the top communications agency of choice.

What Makes Us Unique

We use AI to collect and analyze patients’ medical history. In minutes, users can receive referral to the appropriate healthcare offering from the broad range of choices presented.

We also directly handle the billing process and payment. Not only are patients able to make payments immediately through us, but we can also submit claims to insurance carriers with little input needed from patients or physicians.

Personalized care is very important to us, and we ensure that strong connections are made between patients and clinical teams based on their healthcare needs. Virtual care can be accessed from anywhere, on any device.

We serve patients of all ages, and just as adults are referred to physicians based on their needs, children are connected with pediatricians.

We connect consumers from all over the world, so to overcome language barriers, we use intelligence to match patients and physicians sharing a native language.
